JOURNEY

I have been working in the shipping industry for over 14 years, gaining experience in operations, customer service, and process management. My interest in coding began when I started using VBA to automate repetitive tasks and simplify daily workflows. That early exposure sparked a passion for building tools that solve real-world problems.

In April 2024, I officially began self-studying to become a full stack web developer. I dedicated time outside of work to learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and backend technologies, all with the goal of transitioning into a tech-focused role.

Recently, I successfully made that transition — stepping into a Software Engineer role within the shipping industry. I'm now using my knowledge to build internal tools, streamline operations, and support my team through automation and innovation.

goal is to grow as a developer and build useful, meaningful tools that improve the way people work. I find genuine joy in creating automation that makes life easier for my colleagues — and that’s what keeps me learning and building every day.
